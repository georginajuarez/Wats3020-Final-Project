FAQs

Are you open year-round?
No, we are open mid April to October 31st.

Can I fish at Big Twin Lake?
Yes, selective fishery, single barbless hooks, and one fish limit.

Can I take my boat out on the lake?
Yes, but ONLY electric motors allowed.

Do you have any boats we can rent?
Yes, we have a selection of boat rentals (Paddleboats, Rowboats, and Kayaks).

Is there Wi-Fi at the campground?
Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi for our guests.

Do you accept credit cards?
Yes, we accept Mastercard and Visa.

What is your availability?
